Once Upon a Time...

There was a young Llama bound and determined to make the world more beautiful.

No matter where he was or what he did there was no escaping the colors that swirled in his eyes.

So he searched the planet for the perfect canvas

But he would never find it. Until one fateful night something strange happened.

Startled he quickly awoke from his slumber to check out the commotion. To his suprise there was a device that he'd never seen before. Then as fear of the unknown sweep over him, he pressed the button.

Just like the the device opened up and spewed out more colors then he had ever seen. Painting the sky and his world. Now he could finally rest this this was the prefect canvas.
